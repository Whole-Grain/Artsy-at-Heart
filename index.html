This colorful cow has led her to do more works of this style, and remains to an example of the unique styles she can combine in order to make works of art.

Bringing Out The Artist In You

Painting with Artsy at Heart

Besides creating very cool works of art on her own, Mary is now venturing out to teach others in her community to paint! She hosts these classes once a week, and allows all her pupils to thrive in a caring enviroment with no pressure! Subjects change each week, so no one should get bored in this painting class! These classes are for anyone wanting to learn to paint, whether it be that you're completely new to the painting world, or you're a seasoned veteran looking to touch up on the fundamentals, so don't be shy!

Custom Artwork

Intarsia

Intarsia is a very complex woodworking art that can produce amazing results. Mary has recently picked up this artform, and is able to take requests if you want a piece done in this style. Intarsia is very complex, so naturally, it takes a long time for a piece to come together, but the results are stunngin and well worth the wait. In the piece pictured, "Indian Scouts" is one of the very first pieces she made, and remains and one of the hallmark examples of what can be accomplished with this artform.

Murals

Aside from woodworkings, Mary started out, and still does, murals. If you need a small office decorated with a painting, or if your workshop needs a grand mural to display your wealth, Mary can do it. Mary has many murals around her local area accreditted to her name, and each one speaks volumes about the ability that Mary has with a paintbrush.
